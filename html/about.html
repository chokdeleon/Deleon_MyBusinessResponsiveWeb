THE WEBSITE AUTHOR

AVIEL VIKTOR DE LEON

My name is Aviel Viktor De Leon, a 15-year-old Grade 9 student and the author of Sa'Dahon, a website dedicated to showcasing a local gem in our city. Sa'Dahon centers around a restaurant, owned by my mom and her two best friends. This unique dining spot serves classic Filipino dishes on a piece of banana leaf, celebrating our culinary traditions in a simple yet authentic way. Through my website, I aim to promote Sa'Dahon as a hub of delicious food and cultural pride. As a young creator, I’m passionate about sharing this meaningful part of our heritage with others.

MORE ABOUT SA'DAHON

Sa'Dahon is a Filipino-style restaurant that takes pride in offering authentic and classic Filipino meals served on a piece of banana leaf, a presentation that evokes the warmth and simplicity of traditional Filipino dining. Located at A. Mabini Street, Burgos, Rodriguez Rizal (inside Buckle Up Carwash), this hidden gem offers more than just great food—it’s a journey back to your childhood and a celebration of Filipino culture and heritage.

The restaurant’s interior is designed to transport diners to a nostalgic past, with old wooden windows used as decorations, vintage lamps that exude a timeless vibe, and an overall ambiance that feels both cozy and familiar. Every detail has been carefully curated to create an inviting atmosphere that pairs perfectly with the hearty Filipino meals they serve.

Owned by three best friends—Marivic De Leon, Kathryn Purification, and Angelique Calasang—Sa Dahon is a testament to their shared passion for food, friendship, and Filipino traditions. Established in late 2024, the restaurant has quickly become a favorite spot for families, friends, and anyone looking to enjoy delicious, home-style Filipino cuisine in a setting that feels like home. Whether you’re dropping by after a car wash or making it your destination, Sa Dahon is a place where food and memories are shared and cherished.
